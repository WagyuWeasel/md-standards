## How We Prioritize

<!-- Be transparent about how decisions are made. This builds trust
     and helps contributors understand why their request may not be
     next. -->

We prioritize based on:

1. **User impact** — how many users are affected and how severely
2. **Strategic alignment** — does it move us toward the [vision](#vision)
3. **Effort** — can we deliver meaningful value within a reasonable scope
4. **Dependencies** — does it unblock other high-priority work

Feature requests and feedback are tracked in
[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/[OWNER]/[REPO]/issues). Upvote
issues that matter to you — it helps us prioritize.
